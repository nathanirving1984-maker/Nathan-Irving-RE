About Nathan

Marin County REALTOR®

Coldwell Banker Realty, Greenbrae Office  ·  CA DRE# 02414117

Nathan Irving is a real estate agent with Coldwell Banker Realty's Greenbrae office, serving buyers and sellers throughout Marin County. Raised in Marin, Nathan brings more than professional expertise to the table — he brings a lifetime of firsthand knowledge of the towns, schools, and neighborhoods that make this area home. As a recent college graduate who chose to build his career right where he grew up, Nathan combines genuine local roots with the energy, drive, and fresh perspective of someone just starting out — backed by the training and resources of one of real estate's most trusted names.

What sets Nathan apart isn't years in the business — it's how he works. He's responsive and available when his clients need him, not squeezed in between a decade of other commitments and side priorities. He's fluent in the tools and platforms that actually move real estate today, from digital marketing to modern, tech-forward communication, ensuring listings get maximum visibility and buyers never miss an opportunity in a competitive market.

Nathan measures success by the relationships he builds, not just the transactions he closes. Because he's building his reputation one client at a time, every buyer and seller gets his full attention — there's no coasting on autopilot, and no client treated like just another file. Clients work directly with Nathan from the first conversation through closing day, backed by the full support of Coldwell Banker Realty's training, market data, and professional network.

Local Roots

Growing up here made all the difference.

Growing up in Marin gave Nathan a genuine feel for the region's distinct communities — from Greenbrae to the surrounding towns — and he brings that lived-in perspective to every client interaction, whether guiding a first-time buyer or a longtime local ready for a change.

Global Backing

Local knowledge, global reach.

Nathan proudly represents clients through Coldwell Banker Realty, a globally recognized brokerage with an unmatched referral network and marketing reach. This combination of local roots and international exposure ensures Marin County listings get seen by the right buyers, wherever they are.
